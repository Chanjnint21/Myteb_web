TRUSTWORHTY COMPANY

MYTEB is the premier Ed Tech solutions provider in the Kingdom. We provide top to bottom school solutions for learning and sharing, showing and telling making and doing. We strive to be leaders in offering teaching tools that drive 21st Century learning in the classroom and science labs. MYTEB is the pioneer and largest provider of Google Chromebook laptops in the Kingdom. We are a key education partner of ACER, EPSON and VIEWSONIC in Cambodia. Our Digital Classroom Solutions include Interactive projectors, touch screen whiteboards, learning Tablets, All in one PCs, laptops, STEAM kits and Robotics systems. We also develop school infrastructure including internet and networking solutions, security cameras and gated access points, video walls and School Information Systems (SIS). We bundle our hardware with online learning software for blended learning, flipped classrooms, Learning Management Solutions (LMS) and more. We do this by collaborating with both local and regional experts to offer a complete educational solution. We work with global brands and local vendors to make technology effective and relevant to the local learning environment.​
