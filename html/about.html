WHO ARE WE?

MYTEB is comprised of a team of young professionals, passionate about improving education through the power of technology. We want to implement 21st-Century learning tools and techniques to propel young learners to be the future leaders of Industry 4.0. We believe that when used efficiently, technology can enable teachers to engage students in an effective manner and measurably improve learning outcomes.

OUR VISIONS
And MISIONS

Our vision is to be the leading provider of educational technology solutions in Cambodia.

We aim to provide schools with the tools and resources they need to create a 21st-century learning environment that will prepare students for the challenges of the future.

We believe that by leveraging technology, we can help schools deliver a more engaging and effective learning experience for students, ultimately leading to improved learning outcomes and better educational opportunities for all.
