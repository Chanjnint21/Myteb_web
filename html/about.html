Who are we?

MYTEB is comprised of a team of young professionals, passionate about improving education through the power of technology. We want to implement 21st-Century learning tools and techniques to propel young learners to be the future leaders of Industry 4.0.


We believe that when used efficiently, technology can enable teachers to engage students in an effective manner and measurably improve learning outcomes.
